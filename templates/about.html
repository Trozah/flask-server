03 : About me

The Big Leap...

Hello! My name is Troza Hill, and I am a Software Developer based in the United States. I'm a freelancer working remotely on solving problems for clients all over the world. After six years of insurance sales and management, I decided to change my occupation to pursue my passion for technology and become a software engineer. I enjoy communicating with people and work well in a team. As an insurance agent, I learned what makes the customer experience valuable and how to be an aggressive problem solver. I have been working in business long enough to realize how vital front and back-end web development is to any modern company.

Innovation is my passion...

I primarily develop using Python but have experience with Javascript and the MERN stack. I have over a year of programming experience, and I'm comfortable developing on the front-end or back-end. I am confident that hiring me to work on your next project will be a significant step in the right direction for you. My professional expertise in business and software engineering can provide valuable insights and a fresh perspective to your team.

Fun Fact About Me...

I have always been interested in programming, even before I knew it. I love technology and reading about it! When I'm not coding or spending time with my family, you can find me reading and learning about the latest technology, developer tools, and libraries.
